# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective collaborators, clients, hiring teams, and peers evaluating Arav's work, experience, and technical focus.

## Product Purpose

Present Arav's current role, background, and selected projects in a concise portfolio that makes credibility and contact paths easy to assess.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, technical, and direct. The site should feel quietly confident rather than promotional.

## Anti-references

Avoid card-heavy SaaS landing pages, decorative typography, inflated claims, and visual effects that compete with the work itself.

## Design Principles

- Put the work and experience before decoration.
- Make the page easy to scan without flattening meaningful emphasis.
- Communicate technical credibility with specific, concrete language.
- Preserve a sparse, personal profile rather than a product-marketing shell.
- Use motion and ornament only when they support orientation or identity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable text, strong contrast, semantic structure, keyboard-accessible links, browser zoom support, and a reduced-motion fallback.
